Research

Nima Eslami

Optimization under Uncertainty

My research investigates mathematical models and algorithms for decision-making when problem data are uncertain. My work includes robust optimization, distributionally robust optimization, and two-stage optimization models.

Riemannian Optimization

I study optimization problems whose decision variables belong to nonlinear geometric spaces, including matrix manifolds. My interests include trust-region methods, stochastic algorithms, nonsmooth optimization, and convergence analysis.

Financial Optimization

My research in financial optimization includes portfolio selection, risk budgeting, index tracking, and models that account for uncertainty in asset returns and probability distributions.

Operations Management Applications

I am interested in applications involving supply chain network design, facility location, resource allocation, and resilient decision-making under disruptions and uncertain operating conditions.
