Research

Nima Eslami

Optimization under Uncertainty

My research investigates mathematical models and computational methods for decision-making when problem data are uncertain. My work includes robust optimization, distributionally robust optimization, stochastic optimization, and two-stage optimization models.

I am particularly interested in developing optimization methods that remain reliable when probability distributions, model parameters, or future operating conditions are not known precisely.

Riemannian Optimization

I study optimization problems whose decision variables lie on nonlinear geometric spaces, including matrix manifolds. My interests include Riemannian trust-region methods, stochastic algorithms, nonsmooth optimization, and convergence analysis.

These methods are useful for optimization problems involving orthogonality constraints, low-rank matrix structures, and other geometric restrictions.

Financial Optimization

My research in financial optimization includes portfolio selection, risk budgeting, index tracking, and models that account for uncertainty in asset returns, covariance estimates, and probability distributions.

I am interested in combining mathematical optimization with risk measures and uncertainty-aware models to support more reliable financial decisions.

Multiobjective Optimization

I am interested in optimization problems involving several competing objectives. These problems arise when decision-makers must balance criteria such as cost, risk, efficiency, robustness, and sustainability.

My work considers mathematical formulations and computational methods for identifying efficient trade-offs among conflicting objectives.

Operations Management Applications

I study applications involving supply-chain network design, facility location, resource allocation, and resilient decision-making under disruptions and uncertain operating conditions.

My goal is to develop optimization models that are both theoretically rigorous and practically useful for complex planning and management problems.
