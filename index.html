Nima Eslami

PhD Candidate in Mathematics, University of British Columbia

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at the University of British Columbia, Okanagan campus. My research lies at the intersection of operations research, optimization under uncertainty, and mathematical optimization.

My current research interests include robust optimization, distributionally robust optimization, stochastic optimization, Riemannian optimization, and multiobjective optimization.

I am particularly interested in developing theoretically sound and computationally efficient methods for complex decision-making problems, with applications in finance, supply chain management, facility location, and machine learning.
